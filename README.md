Personal project combining my two favourite things sifting and making sense of data as well as my love for fishing 


Problems faced:
1-Small dataset. Although I have spent over a year collecting the data myself I realized a model will not work  to currently predict anything reliably.
2-So many variables when it comes to fishing!!!

Mindset shift:
My data is still very useful, these catch recordings are done in 2 different estuaries, Durban Harbour and Blue Lagoon in South Africa.
What I can conclude is tidal coefficients do effect how these areas fish differently.
